  • New attendace system

    With our Daily Absences Dashboard, Attendance Officers have a clear overview of any unauthorised absences. By providing them with a clear list of students who are absent that day and easy access to guardian contact details, staff can quickly follow up with parents and identify the reason for absence. Attendance in the current climate can be difficult to track with schools still having to navigate closures whether in part or full. ith Attendance, teachers can take attendance from wherever they are for both in-school and remote learners whilst still communicating absences to colleagues.
